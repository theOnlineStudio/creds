The Brief

Bring to life a homepage takeover on theaa.com to promote their home emergency cover

Creative +

We worked with the The AA to advise the best way to shoot assets, then created a mockup to test out various different types of animation.

Project Management +

As this was the first of its kind on theaa.com, we worked with their production team to adivse best practises in terms of frequency capping and analytics as well as documenting the process to esure future jobs ran smoothly.

Development +

Using multiple components, we were able to create the effect that theaa.com was filling with water, whilst also allowing users direct access to all areas of the page, so as not to interrupt their journey.
