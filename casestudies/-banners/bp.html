The Brief

To create a suite of standard banners (5 sizes of 3 creatives, plus 3 copy variants of each - total 45 banners) and a suite of rich media banners (30 polite loading banners, 5 interactive polite loading banners, 2 synced interactive homepage takeovers and 3 interactive expandables)

Project Management +

Worked for a small agency who worked for a large agency who worked for a large corporation to fulfill this media schedule. However, we managed the resulting long approvals process and Chinese whispers so that the only amends that came back to us were new amends, and our deadlines were met

Development +

At the last minute before launch, the team managed to update all banners to incorporate a new corporate logo and brand colours within the day
